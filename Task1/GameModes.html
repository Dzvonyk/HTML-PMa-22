Information about game modes.

The game has two game modes:

PvE:

  • Puzzles: a set of tactical tasks of varying difficulty levels, each of which reproduces a certain situation on the playing field. The player is supposed to figure out how to destroy an opponent in one move.
  • Campaign: A story mode, accompanied by comics about characters. Currently there are three campaigns in the game. The story of the first Empty Throne campaign tells about five characters that are somehow related to Keifas: the bastard Caleb, the daughter of Wara, Sister Talir, wife Aylin and Uncle Roland. The second Jekk's Bounty Campaign talks about the adventures of Jack's mercenary killer and is a collection of different missions with special conditions. The third - The History of Horus Trever - tells the story of the formation of labeled - the main antagonists of the universe. During the passage of each campaign, the player receives thematic maps in reward.
  • Gauntlet: The player chooses a deck and starts a series of games of 7 fights. The game goes until the first defeat or until the whole series ends. The last battle is a boss with special battle conditions.
  • Forge: The player draws 25 cards, sequentially choosing from the three suggested ones. T hen the game adds the necessary power cards to the deck. It is necessary to win 7 fights, in the second defeat the series ends. Access to paid mode. Selected maps are added to the collection.

PvP:

  • Event: temporary events from the developer
  • Casual: Battle 1 to 1 against the opponent.
  • Ranked: A player passes 5 qualifying matches and then fights for a place in the ranking, earning points for victory and losing them for defeat. At the end of each rating season an appropriate reward is issued. Ranges: bronze, silver, gold, diamond, master.
  • Draft: The player draws cards from several boosters, then they make an option of a XX-card deck. Then the game is played to three defeats or seven wins. Access to paid mode. Selected maps are added to the collection.
